Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API.

Data Storage

The extension stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Flat listing titles
  • Prices, sizes, and room counts
  • Addresses and URLs of saved listings

This data never leaves your browser and is not transmitted to any server.

Permissions

The extension requires access to ImmoScout24.de to:

  • Extract flat information from listing pages
  • Add the "Add to Compare" button to the page

No other websites are accessed.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

Data Deletion

You can delete all stored data at any time by:

  • Clicking "Clear All" in the extension popup
  • Uninstalling the extension
  • Clearing your browser's extension data

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
